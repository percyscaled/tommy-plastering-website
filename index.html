What We Do
Our Services
01
Exterior Stucco
Full exterior stucco applications for homes and commercial buildings. Three-coat systems, one-coat systems, and synthetic stucco (EIFS) installed to South Florida code standards.
02
Interior Plastering
Expert interior plaster finishes from smooth Level 5 to decorative Venetian. We match existing textures seamlessly and create new custom finishes to your exact specification.
03
Stucco Repair
Crack repair, patch work, and full section replacement. We identify underlying moisture issues and fix the root cause before recoating — no shortcuts, no recurring problems.
04
Custom Textures
From Spanish lace to Santa Barbara smooth, Mediterranean bands to modern sand finish. Tommy specialises in complex custom textures that most contractors won't attempt.
05
New Construction
Preferred stucco sub for general contractors across Miami-Dade. We work on tight schedules, coordinate with other trades, and never hold up a project.
06
Commercial Projects
Office buildings, retail spaces, HOA common areas, and multi-family complexes. We handle large-scale commercial stucco with the precision and reliability commercial clients demand.
